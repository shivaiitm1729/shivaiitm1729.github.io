Biography

Shivakumar Balachandran is a PhD Candidate and a Graduate Research Assistant in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of South Carolina. His research focuses on numerical and machine learning models for dam and levee breach floods. Before starting the PhD, he worked on optimizing Water Supply Systems in pipe networks and designing stormwater management. Shivakumar holds an M.Tech. degree, specializing in hydraulics and water resources engineering, from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras (IIT-M) in Chennai, India.

Research Interests

  • Numerical modeling of transient flows in pipes and dam-break flows
  • Machine Learning for levee and dam-break floods
  • Flood inundation mapping for dam-break floods
  • Probabilistic hazard mapping
